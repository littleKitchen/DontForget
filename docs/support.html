Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a gift card?

Tap the + button in the top right corner. You can scan the barcode, enter details manually, and take a photo of the card.

How do I scan a barcode?

When adding a card, tap "Scan Barcode" and point your camera at the barcode. The app supports QR codes, EAN-13, Code128, and most other barcode formats.

Can I show the barcode at checkout?

Yes! Open any card and tap on the barcode to display it full-screen. The screen brightness will automatically increase for easier scanning.

How do location reminders work?

When adding a card, you can link it to a store location. When you're near that location, you'll get a notification reminding you about the card.

How do I update a card's balance?

Open the card details and tap on the balance to edit it. You can also swipe left on a card in the list and tap "Update Balance".

What happens when I mark a card as "Used"?

The card is moved to your used cards history. You can view used cards in Settings if you need to reference them later.

When do I get expiration notifications?

By default, you'll be notified 7 days before a card expires, then 3 days before, and on the day of expiration. You can adjust this in Settings.
