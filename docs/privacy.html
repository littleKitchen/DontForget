Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Overview

Don't Forget ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our gift card tracking application.

Data Collection

Don't Forget does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All your data stays on your device.

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device:

  • Gift card details (store name, balance, barcode)
  • Card photos you take
  • Expiration dates
  • Store locations for reminders
  • App preferences

Camera Access

Don't Forget requests camera access to:

  • Scan barcodes on your gift cards
  • Take photos of your cards for reference

Photos and scanned data are stored only on your device and never uploaded anywhere.

Location Services

Don't Forget may request location access to remind you when you're near a store where you have a gift card. This location data is:

  • Used only for geofence-based reminders
  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to any server

You can deny location access and still use all other features of the app.

Third-Party Services

Don't Forget does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including encryption and biometric locks.

Children's Privacy

Don't Forget does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
